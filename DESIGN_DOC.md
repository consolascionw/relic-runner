# DESIGN_DOC.md

## High Concept
A cozy, top-down farming adventure where players build a vibrant world and defend their homestead from forest creatures. The goal is to provide a relaxing yet engaging experience of growth and exploration.

## Core Design Pillars
- **Visual Cohesion:** Using a consistent 16-bit style to ensure the world feels unified and charming.
- **Responsive Interaction:** Ensuring the player feels immediate feedback when farming or fighting.
- **Readability:** High-contrast pixel art that makes it easy for players to identify interactable crops versus decorative foliage.

## Target Platform and Audience
- **Platform:** PC / Web
- **Audience:** Players who enjoy relaxing simulators like Stardew Valley but with a "cute fantasy" RPG twist.

## Core Gameplay Loop
Gathering Resources -> Building/Farming -> Defending/Exploring -> Upgrading Homestead.

## Mechanics Overview
- **Player:** 8-way movement with actions for farming (hoeing, watering) and combat (3-stage combo attack).
- **Enemy:** Simple AI pathfinding (Slimes) and explosive hazards (Bombshrooms).
- **Hazards:** Environmental obstacles like cliffs and deep water.
- **Relic & Collectibles:** Ores and crops used for progression.

## Level Design
Levels are designed to start in a safe "Home Base" area. Difficulty increases as the player moves further into caves and dense forests where enemies spawn more frequently.

## UI and Feedback
- Heart-based health UI.
- Floating damage numbers.
- Smooth transitions between UI menus.

## Audio and Game Feel
- Relaxing acoustic background music for farming.
- "Squish" sound effects for slime combat.
- Screen shake during bomb explosions.

## Art and Visual Style
We are using the **Cute Fantasy RPG Asset Pack** by **Kenmi-art**. The style is 16-bit, top-down pixel art characterized by vibrant colors and "chibi" proportions.

## Final Design Note
This project follows the provided Game Design Template and aims to deliver a polished vertical slice of a fantasy life-sim.
